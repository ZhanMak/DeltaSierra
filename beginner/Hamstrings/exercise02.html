Hamstrings for Beginners

Starting position:

Find a low platform to elevate the toes and bring the feet close together.

Take the dumbbells out so they’re tracking over the toes instead of keeping them close to the body. Now bend down, keeping your torso and legs straight. Control the eccentric tempo to feel the stretch properly, pause for a second in the bottom position, and hinge back up.

Do 2 sets of 10 reps.
