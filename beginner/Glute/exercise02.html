Glute for Beginners

Starting position:

Stand with your feet together, and then take a step forward and plant your foot firmly to the ground.

From there, start bending your back knee down, so it touches the ground at 90 degrees. While driving through the floor with your front foot, move your body forward to a standing position, where your back foot will meet the position of the front one.

Do 2 sets of 10 steps per leg.
