Quads for Beginners

Starting position:

Lift a kettlebell or dumbbell with both hands, holding it with your thumbs resting against your upper chest.

Maintain an upright posture, keeping your shoulders back and down. Brace your core. Squat down until your thighs break parallel with the floor. Push through your feet to return to standing.

Do 3 sets of 10 reps.
