Quads for Beginners

Starting position:

Grab a pole or something similar.

Take two or three steps back and lean your torso forward at your hips. Drive your knees forward until they are fully flexed, so you are in a sitting position. Allow your heels to come off the ground but don’t move the toes. From the bottom, extend your knees to stand up.

Do 3 sets of 12 reps.
