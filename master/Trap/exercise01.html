Trap for Masters

Starting position:

Stand tall with a dumbbell in each hand and arms at your sides, palms facing in. Position your feet roughly shoulder-width apart. Raise your arms and lift the dumbbells up and out to each side, stopping when your elbows reach around shoulder-height, arms should be 30 degrees in front of the body. Pause and hold for a second at the top of the movement. Control the weight as you return to the starting position.

Do 2 sets of 25 reps.
