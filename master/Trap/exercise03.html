Trap for Masters

Starting position:

Place the knee opposite the arm you’re doing exercise with onto a bench or another stable surface. Place the non-working arm down to support your torso. Make sure your back is straight and allow the free leg to extend behind you or to the side. Pick up the dumbbell with a neutral grip (palms facing toward the body) and row it up, leading with your elbow. Pull the weight in a controlled manner without leaning your body to either side. Repeat on the opposite side.

Do 2 sets of 20 reps on each arm.
