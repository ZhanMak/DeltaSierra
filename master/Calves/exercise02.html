Calves for Masters

Starting position:

Sit up straight with your legs bent at 90 degrees with the balls of your feet on the step and the load above the knee on the thigh, a barbel with weights can be a good example. Slowly lower your heels as far as possible to the floor, pausing for three seconds. Then lift your heels as far as possible, squeezing your calves at the top of the movement.

Do 3 sets of 20 reps.
