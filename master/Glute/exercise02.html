Glute for Masters

Starting position:

Sit with your upper back against a bench and a loaded barbell in front of you. Bend your knees and plant your feet on the floor to seal the bar into your hip crease. Grab the bar with a wide grip. Initiate the thrust by pushing your hips upward until your body forms a straight line from your kneecaps to your shoulders.

Do 5 sets of 10 reps.
