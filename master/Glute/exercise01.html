Glute for Masters

Starting position:

Place barbell on your upper back with a close grip. Take a comfortable stance, place your feet between hip and shoulder-width apart and turn your feet outward to whatever angle feels natural. From here, inhale into your belly and feel your weight distribute evenly across your feet. Squat by bending your knees and hips simultaneously and sitting down as low as you are able. Once you reach the bottom, reverse the motion and stand back up by contracting your quads.

Do 4 sets of 12 reps.
