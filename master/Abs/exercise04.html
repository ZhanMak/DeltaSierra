Abs for Masters

Starting position:

Lie on the floor holding an empty or lightly loaded barbell over your chest as in the top of a bench press. Your legs should be extended on the floor in front of you. Perform a sit-up, raising your torso until it’s vertical. Keep the bar over your head, so it drifts back to an overhead press position at the top of the sit-up.

Do 20 reps.
