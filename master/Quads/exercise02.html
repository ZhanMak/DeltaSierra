Quads for Masters

Starting position:

Stand a few feet in front of a bench or similar. Adjust your position so that when you place your back-foot laces down on the elevated surface, both knees can reach 90 degrees when bent. Grab hold of a weight, put the top of your foot on a bench and stabilize your position. Brace your core and descend into a lunge. Descend until your back leg reaches its full range of motion — your back knee should be pointing down at the ground. Press hard into your front foot to rise back to standing.

Do 3 sets of 15 reps per leg.
