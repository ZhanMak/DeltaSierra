Hamstrings for Masters

Starting position:

Stand tall with your feet hip-distance apart. Grip the barbell with an overhand grip in front of the thighs. Keep your chest up and shoulders down. Take a deep breath in. Hinge until the barbell is just below your knees. Always keep the barbell close to your body. Keep your lats engaged. Squeeze your glutes to hinge back to a standing position.

Do 20 reps.
