Triceps for Masters

Starting position:

Start by lying back down on a bench or similar, with the hands supporting a weight at the top of the bench-pressing position.

Pull the elbows back slightly so that they are pointing behind you as you bend the elbow joint, lowering the bar handle or loads towards your head. The bar should nearly contact the forehead. Push the bar back up.

Do 15 to 30 reps depending on the weight.
