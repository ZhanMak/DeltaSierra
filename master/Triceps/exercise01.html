Triceps for Masters

Starting position:

Grab the parallel bars with or put your hands on something similar with your torso upright as you are suspended.

Have your elbows almost fully extended to support this position. With the chest up and shoulder blades squeezed together, bend at the elbows as you lower yourself downward until the elbows reach 90 degrees. Press yourself upwards until you fully extend the elbows and repeat.

Do 10 reps two times.
