Hamstrings for Middles

Starting position:

Kneel on both knees.

Secure your ankles with equipment or have a partner hold them for you. Engage your hamstrings, glutes, and abs. Keep your back straight. Slowly lean forward, using only your hamstrings until you catch yourself with your hands or gently reach the ground. Push yourself back up.

Do 3 sets of 10 reps.
