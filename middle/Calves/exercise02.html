Calves for Middles

Starting position:

Hold two dumbbells at arm’s length.

Put your front foot on a box around a knee height. Move your back foot behind your hips. Keep the front knee bent at 90 degrees and point both toes forward. Then, lean forward onto the front foot and maintain that position. Perform a calf raise on the back foot, lifting your heel as high as you can. Slowly lower down, reset, and repeat.

Do 2 sets of 20 reps for each leg.
