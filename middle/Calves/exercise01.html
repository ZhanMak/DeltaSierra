Calves for Middles

Starting position:

Hold a dumbbell in one hand, secure the other hand, and put the ball of your foot on the raised surface.

Put your other foot behind the working leg and slowly lower it towards the floor, so the working leg gets a good stretch. Then lift your working heel as high as you can and pause and squeeze at the top of the movement. Slowly lower down and repeat.

Do 3 sets of 15 reps for each leg.
