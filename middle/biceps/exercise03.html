Biceps for Middles

Starting position:

Grab a pair of dumbbells or similar and let them hang at arm’s length next to your sides.

Turn your arms so your palms face forward. Without moving your upper arms, bend your elbows and lift the dumbbells as close to your shoulders as you can. Pause for a second, then rotate the dumbbells so your palms face forward again. Slowly lower the weights down in that position. Rotate the dumbbells back to the starting position.

Do 15 reps.
