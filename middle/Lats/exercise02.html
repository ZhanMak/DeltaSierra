Lats for Middles

Starting position:

Set up an adjustable bench to 30-45 degrees.

Lie face down on the bench and hold the dumbbells with a neutral grip (palms facing each other). Drive your elbows back towards your hips until your elbows come in line with your torso, pull your back up at the same time. Aim to keep your elbows by your sides as you pull. Control the weight back to full extension of the arms, allowing the dumbbell to pull you into a full stretch.

Do 15 reps.
