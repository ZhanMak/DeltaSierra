Glute for Middles

Starting position:

Stand with your feet around shoulder width apart with a dumbbell or kettlebell or similar weight and hold it directly under your chin with your elbows tucked in.

Brace your core, tense your back, and ensure that you feel stable. Keep your chest up and squat down until both of your legs bend to around 90 degrees. Stand back up.

Do 3 sets of 16 reps.
