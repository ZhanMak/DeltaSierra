Chest for Middles

Starting position:

Get into a plank position, with your hands underneath your shoulders, back flat, and feet together.

Screw your palms into the ground, so the elbows are close to the body. Hold this position, and then slowly lower yourself until your chest is about an inch from the floor. Now, get back up.

Do 18 unbroken reps, rest for 30 seconds and do a set of 8 reps.
