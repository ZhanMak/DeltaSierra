Chest for Middles

Starting position:

Start by taking two smaller weight plates or similar and pressing them together between your hands.

Your arms should be extended outwards in front of you. While actively pinching the plate together and not letting them slip apart, pull the plates towards your sternum as you keep your chest up and shoulder blades pulled together. Flex your chest and press the weights back outwards. Keep the plates pressed together and the inner chest muscles engaged.

Do 5 slow reps.
