Shoulders for Middles

Starting position:

Start in the top position of a pushup with your hands on the floor, shoulder-width apart.

Take one dumbbell or something similar in your hand, row it toward the side of your body while balancing on the opposite hand and feet. Pause for one second at the top and return the weight slowly to the start position. Repeat on the other side.

Do 20 repeats overall, choose a light to medium weight.
